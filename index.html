About us

Time to learn who we are..

Office 3 Sixty is a office automation supplier that evolved from a communications and internet provider. Over time, the market became saturated, which forced us into thinking “sustainability”. We conducted market research and discovered that business infrastructure and solution architecture were highly sought after in this modern and tech-driven day and age. This is how and when we started providing businesses with their most valued infrastructure, which would allow them to excel, automate processes and focus on growing their turnover. We specialise in designing solutions for various environments, eliminating the need for businesses to waste valuable time on designing, procuring and implementing various solutions. Through our partnerships with various experts in various industries, we are able to source almost anything a business requires to function efficiently.
